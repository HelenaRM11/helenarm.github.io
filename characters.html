RPG Game - Altar Cove

The game, provisionally named Altar Cove, is a life simulator role-playing game. This is a game where the player finds themselves turned into an animal hybrid because of a potion accident. The player has no previous experience with withccraft, but because of this incident he moves to a town full of witches and wizards. Slowly, the player will learn to use more and more magic until they are able to reverse the accident that made them into a hybrid.

Combat game - Untitled

This is a combat and exploration third-person game with an urban fantasy setting. Aspyn, a werewolf who is a professional fighter, goes looking for her cousin, Roland. Roland has gone missing in mysterious circumstances and Aspyn will have to go around the city winning fights and sorting puzzles or avoiding the conflicts of the two local, rival street gangs.

Survival Game - Wake Up

This game, provisionally named Wake Up, is a first-person survival and suspense game where the player finds themselves locked inside Alex’s mind after a car accident. Everything is in disarray, and the player, helped by the voice of Alex’s best friend, Julia, will have to solve puzzles to put everything back into order.

Platformer Game - Untitled

This is a platformer game with a ronin main character who will slowly learn and gain powers relating to the four elements. Yaeko, the main character, is trying to rescue a young girl, Haruko, whom she had been tasked to protect. With a tragic backstory but a fiery practicality, Yaeko runs after Haruko and her captors when she is kidnapped during an ambush.
