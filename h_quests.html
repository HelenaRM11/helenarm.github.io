Main Quest 4: Where's Roland?

Game Context

This is a combat and exploration third-person game with an urban fantasy setting. Aspyn, a werewolf who is a professional fighter, goes looking for her cousin, Roland.

Roland has gone missing in mysterious circumstances and Aspyn will have to go around the city winning fights and sorting or avoiding the conflicts of the two local, rival street gangs.

Quest Context

This Quest happens in an illegal fighting club. There are people cheering, booing, and betting on the fighters. Aspyn does not feel uncomfortable in a place like this, and the exploration is calm. But the combat requires a slightly frenetic rhythm, where the player feels the threat of the other fighter.

Condition to Unlock

You must complete quest 3.

Entry Requirements

None, this is an open quest.

Stages Used

Stage nº6 – The Cage

NPCs Used

Raine, Hannas, Devyn, Tam

Mission Description

After finding Roland’s apartment destroyed, Aspyn heads to the Cage to talk to Hannas, the bookie. There, she will have to explore the space to find more information on Roland’s whereabouts. Moreover, she will be able to talk to several characters, but she will find herself dragged into the fights if she wants to be taken seriously.

Important items

In this mission, the player can find the following objects:

• Black boxing wraps: on one of the benches in the locker room.

• Wooden wolf figurine: inside an open locker.

• Betting records: on Hannas’ desk.

Success conditions

The player must find information, either partial or full, about Roland’s whereabouts.

Failiure conditions

This mission can be failed in case the player does not find any information. That is to say, if the player does not complete the puzzle and loses the combat.

Rewards

• White MMA gloves: if the player wins the fight, Tam offers them up.

• Roland’s betting notebook: if the player completes the puzzle, they find the notebook inside of a locker.
