Main Quest 4: Where's Roland?

Game Context

This is a combat and exploration third-person game with an urban fantasy setting. Aspyn, a werewolf who is a professional fighter, goes looking for her cousin, Roland.

Roland has gone missing in mysterious circumstances and Aspyn will have to go around the city winning fights and sorting or avoiding the conflicts of the two local, rival street gangs.

Quest Context

This Quest happens in an illegal fighting club. There are people cheering, booing, and betting on the fighters. Aspyn does not feel uncomfortable in a place like this, and the exploration is calm. But the combat requires a slightly frenetic rhythm, where the player feels the threat of the other fighter.

Condition to Unlock

You must complete quest 3.

Entry Requirements

None, this is an open quest.

Stages Used

Stage nº6 – The Cage

NPCs Used

Raine, Hannas, Devyn, Tam

Mission Description

After finding Roland’s apartment destroyed, Aspyn heads to the Cage to talk to Hannas, the bookie. There, she will have to explore the space to find more information on Roland’s whereabouts. Moreover, she will be able to talk to several characters, but she will find herself dragged into the fights if she wants to be taken seriously.

Important items

In this mission, the player can find the following objects:

• Black boxing wraps: on one of the benches in the locker room.

• Wooden wolf figurine: inside an open locker.

• Betting records: on Hannas’ desk.

Success conditions

The player must find information, either partial or full, about Roland’s whereabouts.

Failiure conditions

This mission can be failed in case the player does not find any information. That is to say, if the player does not complete the puzzle and loses the combat.

Rewards

• White MMA gloves: if the player wins the fight, Tam offers them up.

• Roland’s betting notebook: if the player completes the puzzle, they find the notebook inside of a locker.

Main Quest 1: Welcome to Altar Cove

Game Context

The game, provisionally named Altar Cove, is a life simulator role-playing game. This is a game where the player finds themselves turned into an animal hybrid because of a potion accident.

The player has no previous experience with withccraft, but because of this incident he moves to a town full of witches and wizards. Slowly, the player will learn to use more and more magic until they are able to reverse the accident that made them into a hybrid.

Quest Context

This is the first quest of the game and serves as a presentation. After a short introduction of the town and the townsfolk, Aline guides the player to talk to Nona, the local ghost, in hopes that she’ll be able to point them towards the first steps in their mission.

This way, the player can lightly explore the town and the different stages while he searches for the Memorial, which Nona usually haunts.

Condition to Unlock

This quest appears immediately after the introduction of the game.

Entry Requirements

None, this is an open quest.

Stages Used

Stage nº1 – Home Stage nº2 – Memorial

NPCs Used

Aline, Nona

Mission Description

This quest must feel warm and comfortable, but also slightly uncertain, since the player and main character have just arrived at the town of Altar Cove.

The music during the quest will be a variation of the music for the rest of the game that uses lower tones and a slower rhythm.

Important items

The important items for this quest are the ones Nona offers at the end:

• One (1) Jade crystal

• One (1) cauldron

Success conditions

Player must find the Memorial stage and talk to Nona.

Failiure conditions

This quest cannot be failed.

Rewards

• One (1) Jade crystal

• One (1) cauldron
