Altar Cove - Town Hall Dialogue

This dialogue is part of an RPG game provisionally called Altar Cove. The dialogue is opened when the player interacts with Aline at the reception desk in the Town Hall. Through it, the player can access different stores and menus. This way they can buy and sell items, adopt or take care of a familiar, and connect with other players. Each of the sisters offers a different possibility.

It is recommended to open the XMind file in a new window, as it's easier to read.


Altar Cove - First Quest Dialogue

This dialogue is part of an RPG game provisionally called Altar Cove. It is part of the very first quest of the game and it serves as the introduction to the gameplay. Aline welcomes the player to Altar Cove and gives them a brief description of the playable stages and characters. She also directs the player to finding the very first items they will need in order to the magical mishap they have been a victim of.

It is recommended to open the XMind file in a new window, as it's easier to read.


Combat Game - Quest Dialogue

The dialogues in this file belong to a quest for an urban fantasy combat game. In it, the main character interacts with different people in her efforts to find out more information about her brother's whereabouts. This is not only one single dialogue, but multiple ones dealing with the different characters the player can encounter in this particular quest.
